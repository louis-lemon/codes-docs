## EurekaCodes Chat APP Service Application Guide

- To apply for services, you'll need to set up your project first.
- For detailed instructions, please go through **Quick start** first.


### 1. Go to your workspace

![ENG image](https://image.lemoncloud.io/1c240d40-87e4-4ec1-9fa5-f05a354e8dbc)

- Within your project, you can apply for and use various services.
- When you click the "Add Service" button, it will take you to the service catalog screen.
![ENG image](https://image.lemoncloud.io/756d52fe-f5d1-4bc9-9cb5-a289d2d6022c)

- You can apply for the services you need from the catalog list.
- For this example, please select the "eureka-chats-front" service, which is a chat app service.
![ENG image](https://image.lemoncloud.io/0d3221b4-03de-4193-a36c-002f51ed0b6f?width=1024)

- You can review detailed information about the catalog (service).
- Click the "Add Service" button at the bottom to submit your service application.
![ENG image](https://image.lemoncloud.io/4a61bf7d-7fdd-4735-8b13-25b555f85dd5)

- You can review your service application information.


## 2. Checking Applied Projects

![ENG image](https://image.lemoncloud.io/8785342f-ca01-4910-bcb6-87dfa58c0d01)

- You can check the services that have been added to your project.
- Depending on the service you applied for, additional services may be installed.
- Deployment begins as soon as you apply, and it may take about 3-5 minutes to complete.
![ENG image](https://image.lemoncloud.io/ff3f3667-4a16-42fc-a62e-fd868b809d65)

- Once deployment is complete, the status will change to "in-use."
- An "Open Service" button will appear on the right side of the "eureka-chats-front" service.
- Click the "Open Service" button to access the chat service.


![ENG image](https://image.lemoncloud.io/d4eba83e-dc98-42f8-985a-99d48a89e925)

- This service runs in your own separate cloud environment.
