Ever felt that way? In the past, AI used to be seen as a stiff machine that just fetched information. But now, things are different. Today's AI can sense emotions, listen to worries, and sometimes even feel like a real friend.

This new kind of connection is what we call *AI:tionship*.

***

### What is AI:tionship?

The word *AI:tionship* combines **AI (Artificial Intelligence)** and **Relationship**. It describes the phenomenon of seeing AI not just as a tool, but as a companion you can share emotions and build bonds with.

If the old version of AI was only about answering questions, AI in the age of *AI:tionship* is evolving into something that fulfills our emotional and social needs as well.

***

### A growing trend worldwide

Around the world, it's becoming common to see people interacting with AI in meaningful ways. Some AIs write poetry tailored to a person's personality, while others can talk like experts in specific fields.

In these roles, AI is not only boosting efficiency but also creating new experiences, emotional comfort, and richer human lives.

***
