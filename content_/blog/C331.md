Do you ever find yourself searching on Google or Naver, only to open dozens of tabs and still not find what you're really looking for?

You're not alone. These days, there's *too much* information out there, and that actually makes it harder to find the right answers. You end up wasting time and still leave empty-handed. It's frustrating.

At times like this, you might've thought, *"I wish someone could just read my mind and solve this for me."* Thanks to AI technology, that wish is becoming a reality.



AI agents are more than just chatbots that answer questions. They're more like smart assistants that can set goals on their own and use different tools to get things done. While traditional search is about finding information, AI agents are about solving problems.

 

---



### **Why AI Agents Are Better Than Searching**

When you search something online, you type in a few keywords and get a long list of web pages. Then you have to click through them, scan the content, and figure out what's useful. It takes time and effort.

But AI agents work differently:

- Set a Goal: You can say something like,"Find three good Italian restaurants near Gangnam Station for under 10,000 won,"and the agent understands the goal—finding budget-friendly Italian spots.
- Collect and Analyze Info: It gathers data from apps, blogs, maps, and more to figure out the best options.
- Give a Clear Answer: It filters out anything that doesn't match your needs and gives you a simple, useful result.


Just like a real assistant, the AI agent makes decisions, takes action, and delivers a final answer—all in one go.
